crashterizer

Turn your images into NES glitch art (sort of).
Takes an image, squashes it, does some pointless "magic",
and maps the resulting image to 8x8 chunks of graphics from
Super Mario Bros's CHR. Then stores it for everyone to see.

The original images aren't saved, only the results.
